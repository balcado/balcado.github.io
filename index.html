Okay... so, what really is Balcado?

What's the deal with Balcado, you might ask? Well truth be told, its just a name without any deep meaning - just a quirky label, if you will.

Anyway, Balcado kicked off its journey on 28/3/2023, initialy as a Minecraft-only venture. However, Z_maro, the visionary behind it, decided to expand its horizons into a full-fledged gaming organization. Despite facing setbacks, including bankruptcy, Z_maro's perseverance led to the birth of Balcado¬≤. With renewed determination, he harbors aspirations for Balcado to become the premier gaming organization.


On this server we believe in F2P mechanics; not P2W, thats why we have 2 currencies, the 1st is the Lira (you can get this from doing tasks like mining, farming, fishing, and killing mobs), and the 2nd is the Drachma (which you can get by trading for it using items such as blocks or resources with other players, you can also get this by buying a one-time purchase of one of the donating tiers and getting an X amount of Drachmas a week).
